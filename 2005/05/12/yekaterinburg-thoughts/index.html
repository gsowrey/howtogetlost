Yekaterinburg Thoughts

Filed under: Russia,Uncategorized — Amy @ 10:44 pm

Keep in mind that I’m writing this from Irkutsk, several days and a lifetime later, but Yekaterinburg is largely a blur to me. We spent our last afternoon – our only afternoon – there doing the walking tour from the Lonely Planet guide. I saw a lot of nice buildings, all of which are historic. Can I remember a single one?

Not really.

But I was there.

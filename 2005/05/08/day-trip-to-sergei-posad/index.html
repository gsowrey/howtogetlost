Day trip to Sergei Posad

Filed under: Russia,Uncategorized — Amy @ 6:08 am

Having enough of Moscow already, we headed out to Sergei Posad, formerly known as Zagorsk. One of the “Golden Ring” towns, Sergei Posad is only and hour and a half out of Moscow by wooden-benched passenger train. Fairly uncomfortable but only when packed to the gills as ours was both coming and going.

But it was worth it!

We spent most of our day exploring the walled monastery in the town.

For women, you must wear a head scarf to go in any of the buildings, out of respect. I knew this but still left mine at home, so I picked up a Russian scarf in the local market for around $10. It’s quite pretty, but something about wearing it made it easy to sport a stoic scowl.

What I liked most of all were the large variety of colours, shapes, and styles found on the various buildings.
